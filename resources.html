March 2021: Literacy Foundations and Organizations

This month we would like to focus on organizations and foundations addressing illeracy at a young age as well as with adults. Approximately 45 million Americans are functionally illerate and cannot read above fifth grade level and half of adults cannot read a book written at an eighth-grade level(Literacy Project). We want to help those who are harmed by lack of access and education. There are ways to help literacy learners and one of the ways you can help is donate money or time to literacy organizations and foundations. We are also in the process of organizing an event for spreading awareness and opportunities to help youth and adult literacy learners.
